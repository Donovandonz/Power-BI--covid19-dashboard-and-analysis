## 📌 Project Overview

This project provides a detailed analysis of the COVID-19 pandemic's global impact, tracking cases, deaths, testing rates, and mortality across continents and countries. The dashboards offer insights into the pandemic's progression and help identify patterns in testing effectiveness and death rates.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **📗 Excel** | Data cleaning, transformation, and initial exploration |
| **📊 Power BI** | Interactive dashboard creation, DAX calculations, and visualization |

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- **Global Statistics**: Real-time tracking of total cases, deaths, tests, and death rates
- **Continental Breakdown**: Analysis across all six continents
- **Top 10 Countries**: Ranking of most affected countries by total cases
- **Mortality Analysis**: Death rates and mortality patterns by region
- **Testing Insights**: Testing volumes and positivity rates worldwide

## 🔍 Key Findings & Insights

### 🌎 Global Impact
1. **Total Cases**: 705 million confirmed cases worldwide
2. **Total Deaths**: 7 million lives lost (0.995% global death rate)
3. **Testing Volume**: 7.03 billion tests conducted globally

### 📈 Regional Analysis

#### Highest Case Burden
| Continent | Cases | % of Global |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Europe | 253M | 35.9% |
| Asia | 221M | 31.4% |
| North America | 132M | 18.7% |

#### Highest Mortality Rates
| Continent | Death Rate | Key Insight |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Africa | 2.013% | Highest death rate despite lowest cases |
| South America | 1.948% | Second highest mortality |
| North America | 1.286% | Above global average |

#### Lowest Mortality Rates
| Continent | Death Rate | Key Insight |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Oceania | 0.222% | Best outcomes, isolated geography |
| Asia | 0.701% | Below global average |
| Europe | 0.829% | Slightly below global average |

### 🧪 Testing Insights

#### Testing Leaders
- **Europe** leads in testing volume (4.5M average tests)
- **North America** second (2.6M average tests)
- **Asia** third (1.9M average tests)

#### Testing Positivity Rate
| Continent | Positivity Rate | Interpretation |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Africa | 0.06106% | Highest positivity,可能 under-testing |
| Europe | 0.00278% | Moderate positivity |
| Asia | 0.00097% | Low positivity |
| Oceania | 0.00000% | Effective containment |

### 🏆 Country-Level Insights

#### Top 5 Most Affected Countries
1. **United States**: 112M cases (15.9% of global total)
2. **India**: 45M cases (6.4% of global total)
3. **France**: 40M cases (5.7% of global total)
4. **Germany**: 39M cases (5.5% of global total)
5. **Brazil**: 39M cases (5.5% of global total)

#### Key Observations
- **US alone** accounts for nearly 16% of global cases
- **Top 10 countries** represent ~60% of global cases
- **European dominance**: 4 European countries in top 10
